Got Questions?

Common Questions

Do I own the song after purchase?

Yes — 100%. Full rights transfer upon delivery. You get a written release with every order. Record, release, publish, monetize — no restrictions.

Can I request a specific style or genre?

Absolutely. I write across Pop, R&B, Hip-Hop, Afrobeats, Gospel, Country, Soul, EDM and more. Share references, moods, or a concept — I'll make it fit.

What if I don't love the first draft?

Every package includes revisions — 1 with The Seed, 3 with The Anthem, unlimited with The Legacy. Tell me what's off and I'll rework it until it's right.

Do you ghostwrite without taking credit?

Yes. Add the Ghostwrite + NDA add-on. I sign a confidentiality agreement and waive all credits. The world only knows it as yours.

Can you write to an existing beat?

Of course. Send the file and I'll write directly to the track — matching the energy, tempo, and emotional pocket of your production.
